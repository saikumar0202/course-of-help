About us

Course of help is a registered non profit/Non governmental organisation was found in 2015. It promotes empathy and compassion for all living beings and care for the environment by educating young people through its humane education programs and workshops.

We also Collabrate with different organisation who have similar goals.

Vision

To protect nature by enhancing its flora and fauna, to protect animals and to empower people on various issues.

Mission

To rescue, adopt and rehabilitate animals, To create sustainable, well being, climate resilence environment by promoting the environment education and awareness. Inclusion of works for the social development and integration of underprivileged individuals, groups and communities.

Objectives / Goals

  • To protect the environment by creating awareness to the society in the importance of planting and saving tress, express our concern to the environment.
  • To raise human perceptions of the moral status of animals by recognizing animals as sentient beings.
  • We are dedicated to rescuing, treating, rehabilitating and caring for all animals.
  • To bring about a change consciousness regarding animals and nature, especially for stray, neglected, abandoned or abused animals.
  • To develop regional programmes for environmental sustainability.
  • Inclusion of works for the social development and integration of underprivileged individuals, groups and communities.
  • To collaborate with other NGO's working towards similar goals.
  • To raise sufficient funds to facilitate the achievement of our stated aims.

We believe that every person has the right to access resources and opportunities in order to live and develop with dignity and to become an active and contributing member of our society.
